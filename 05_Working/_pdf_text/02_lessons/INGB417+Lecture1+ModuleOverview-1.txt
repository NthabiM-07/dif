                                How do I FAIL…
• It’s not about enough content...it’s about clever logical simple DESIGN.
• Design is an Art and a Science – so you can be creative, but stay logical and
  practical

• It’s not about just the rubric, it’s about your attitude, the way you talk and
  explain, level of detail you really understand etc.

• It is about the WORDS and PICS you use to communicate your ideas. Not
  communicating effectively and not including details (but remember more is not
  always better, so communicate well)

• Thinking you can depend on buddies or AI, just NO, but you can learn from
  buddies in the GROUP assignment

• Not planning and using your TIME effectively, there are a lot of work to do...do
  not underestimate it!

•   You need to understand movement between groups
•   You need to understand scale…making 1000 vs 10 000
•   – group together
•   You need to think logically!
•   You need to understand floor spaces and open spaces
•   You need to think about the inside of a building but also the outside logistics, how to get
    there, where to park and the flow of people logic
